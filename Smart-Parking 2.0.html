How It Works

1️⃣ Search Parking

Finding a parking space has never been
easier. Our Smart Parking System allows our users to search nearby locations in real time using intelligent tracking and live availability updates. This helps reduce traffic congestion and saves valuable time for drivers.

2️⃣ Check Availability

Users can instantly view available, occupied, and reserved parking slots through our real-time database system. The information gets updated automatically to ensure complete accuracy. This transparency allows our users to make quick and confident decisions.

3️⃣ Book Slot

With just a few clicks, our users can securely reserve their preferred parking space for a selected time period. Our system properly ensures that the slot remains reserved only for the user. Our Authentication and booking confirmation make the process reliable and safe for the user.

4️⃣ Scan & Enter

At the parking entrance, users can scan a QR code or they can use the Automatic Number Plate Recognition for verification. The system validates bookings instantly without manual checking. his enables smooth and completely contactless vehicle entry.

5️⃣ Exit Smoothly

Once the parking session ends, the system automatically calculates the duration of the session and billing amount. The Payments are processed securely and efficiently. Users can exit the parking area quickly without any unnecessary waiting.
